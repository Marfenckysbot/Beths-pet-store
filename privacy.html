Privacy Policy

Your privacy is critically important to us.

At Beth's Pet Store, we are committed to protecting your privacy. We only collect personal information (such as name and email) that you voluntarily provide when you sign up for our newsletter or contact our support. This information is used solely for communicating with you and improving our services.

We implement industry-standard security measures to safeguard your data. We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience on our site, but you can disable cookies through your browser settings if you prefer.

Our website may contain links to external sites (e.g., payment processors, social media). Please note that we are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites. We encourage you to read the privacy policies of any linked websites.

If you have any questions about our privacy policy or how we handle your data, please contact us at marfenckys@yahoo.com.
